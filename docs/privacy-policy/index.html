English

In short: Mission Control Clock does not collect, sell, or share personal data. Clock settings remain under the user's control. Internet access is used only for optional time synchronization.

This Privacy Policy applies to the Mission Control Clock Android application (package br.com.pu2ptu.missioncontrolclock), developed by Mario Pin - PU2PTU.

1. Data collected by the developer

The developer does not collect, receive, store, sell, or share personal information or app usage data. The app has no user accounts, advertising, analytics, tracking technologies, or in-app purchases.

2. Information stored for app operation

Your selected language, clock labels, UTC offsets, display preferences, and synchronization preference are stored as app settings. They are not transmitted to the developer. You can delete these settings by clearing the app's data or uninstalling it.

3. Internet time synchronization

When internet synchronization is enabled or manually requested, the app may contact time.cloudflare.com and pool.ntp.org using the Network Time Protocol (NTP). If NTP is unavailable, it may contact https://www.google.com/generate_204 over HTTPS and read the response date header.

As with any internet connection, those services or participating NTP server operators can receive technical connection information such as the device's public IP address and request time. No clock labels, configured UTC offsets, or other app settings are included in these requests. The developer does not receive or retain the connection information. Third-party processing is governed by the relevant provider's practices, including the Cloudflare Privacy Policy, the Google Privacy Policy, and the practices of servers participating in the NTP Pool Project.

4. Permissions

The app requests Android's Internet permission solely for time synchronization. It does not request access to precise or approximate location, contacts, camera, microphone, photos, or files.

5. Children

The app is a general technical utility and is not directed to children under 13. The developer does not knowingly collect personal information from children or any other users.

6. This policy and third-party hosting

This page is publicly hosted by GitHub Pages. Visiting this webpage is subject to the GitHub General Privacy Statement. This does not add analytics or tracking to the Android app.

7. Changes

This policy may be updated if the app's features or legal requirements change. The effective date at the top of this page will be revised when material changes are made.

8. Contact

For privacy or support questions, contact Mario Pin - PU2PTU at mario.pu2ptu@gmail.com.
